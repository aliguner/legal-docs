# Privacy Policy for CornerBuddy

**Last updated: April 2026**

CornerBuddy values your privacy. This application is designed to function primarily offline, prioritizing the security of your business data.

### 1. Data Storage (SQLite & Disk Access)
All data entered into CornerBuddy (inventory records, sales data, etc.) is stored **locally on your device** in an optimized SQLite database. We do not transmit, sync, or backup your data to any external servers. Users are responsible for their own data management and backups.

### 2. Camera Usage
CornerBuddy requires access to your device's camera to:
* Scan product barcodes for quick inventory management.
* Attach product photos to your local records.
The camera is only active when you initiate these features, and no images are shared outside the app.

### 3. File Access
The app requires permission to read/write to your device's storage to maintain the database and store any photos you take within the app.

### 4. Third-Party Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. Since all data stays on your device, we have no access to it.
